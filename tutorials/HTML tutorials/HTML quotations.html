HTML Quotations and Citation Elements

Quotation / Kutipan

Here is a quote from WWF's website:

For 50 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature. The world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by 1.2 million members in the United States and close to 5 million globally.

HTML <q> for Short Quotations

Elment <q> mendefinisikan kutipan pendek.

Browser biasanya menyisipkan tandai kutip disekitar element <q>.

Contoh

Input:

<p>WWF's goal is to: <q>Build a future where people live in harmony with nature.</q></p>

WWF's goal is to: Build a future where people live in harmony with nature.

HTML <blockquote> for Long Quotations

Element <blockquote> mendefinisikan sebuah sesi yang dikutip.

Browser biasanya membuat jarak (indent).

Contoh

Input:

<p>Here is a quote from WWF's website:</p>
<blockquote cite="http://www.worldwildlife.org/who/index.html">
For 50 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature.
The world's leading conservation organization,
WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by
1.2 million members in the United States and
close to 5 million globally.
</blockquote>

Here is a quote from WWF's website:

For 50 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature. The world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by 1.2 million members in the United States and close to 5 million globally.

HTML <abbr> for Abbreviation

Element <abbr> mendefinisikan sebuah singkatan atau ringkasan suatu kata.

Penandaan singkatan bisa memberikan informasi yang berguna untuk browser, translation system (sistem penterjemah) dan seacrh engine (mesin pencari).

Contoh

Input:

<p>The <abbr title="World Health Organization">WHO</abbr> was founded in 1948.</p>

The WHO was founded in 1948.

HTML <address> for Contact Information

Element address> mendefinisikan informasi kontak (penulis atau pemilik) dsei sebuah dokumen atau artikel.

Element <address> biasanya ditsmpilkan dalam bentuk huruf italic. Kebanyakan browser akan menambahkan sebuah line break (garis baru) sesudah dan sebelum element.

Contoh

Input:

<address>
Written by John Doe.<br>
Visit us at: <br>
Example.com<br>
Box 564, Disneyland<br>
USA
</address>
Written by John Doe.
Visit us at:
Example.com
Box 564, Disneyland
USA

HTML <cite> for Work Title

Element <cite> mendefinisikan judul suatu pekerjaan.

Browser biasanya menampilkan element <cite> dalam bentuk italic.

Contoh

Input:

<p>The HTML cite element defines the title of a work.</p>
<p>Browsers usually display cite elements in italic.</p>

<img src="../../pictures/Rain Fall.png" width="220" height="277" alt="The Scream">
<p><cite>The Scream</cite> by Edvard Munch. Painted in 1893.</p>

The HTML cite element defines the title of a work.

Browsers usually display cite elements in italic.

The Scream by Edvard Munch. Painted in 1893.

HTML <bdo> for Bi-Directional Override

Element <bdo> mendefinisikan bi-directional override (metode penulisan atau kanan atau kiri).

Contoh

Input:

<bdo dir="rtl">This text will be written from right to left</bdo>
This text will be written from right to left
